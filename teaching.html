I'm an assistant professor at the Department of Civil, Chemical, and Environmental Engineering (DICCA) of the University of Genoa. These are the courses that I am currently teaching:
  • Turbulence and CFD Models.
  • Introduction to General CFD Using OpenFOAM Technology with an Overview of Turbulence Modelling and Multiphase Flows.
  • Advanced CFD Course Using OpenFOAM Technology.

I am also co-instructor of the following graduate courses:
  • Aerodynamics (numerical labs using XFOIL, XFLR5, and Ansys Fluent). This course is delivered together with Prof. Alessandro Bottaro.
    • Teaching and support material used in the numerical labs.
  • Advanced Fluid Dynamics (numerical labs using Ansys Fluent and DAKOTA). This course is delivered together with Prof. Jan Pralits.
  • Computational Chemical Engineering - Basis of Computational Techniques (numerical labs using Ansys Fluent). This is course is delivered together with Prof. Jan Pralits.
