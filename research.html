Research

My main areas of research are:
  • Multi-physics simulations, computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and scientific computing.
  • Mesh generation and solid modeling.
  • Theoretical and computational aerodynamics.
  • Aerodynamics of incompressible flows and compressible flows.
  • Turbulence modeling.
  • Multiphase flows (separated and dispersed flows).
  • CFD for Architecture, Engineering and Construction (AEC).
  • Thermal comfort.
  • High-performance computing and cloud computing.
  • Flight mechanics and aircraft stability.
  • Scientific visualization and augmented reality.
  • Uncertainty quantification.
  • Design optimization, design space exploration, and surrogate-based optimization for engineering design.
  • Data analytics, exploratory data analysis, and interactive data visualization.
  • Machine learning and data mining.
  • Image and pattern recognition.
  • Sports analytics and econometrics.

Feel free to send me an email if you are interested in collaborating in one of these fields or if you are a student and you are looking for an exciting research subject.
